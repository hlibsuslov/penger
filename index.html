[ HOW ENCODING WORKS ]

From Words to Titanium.
A Complete Guide.

Every BIP39 seed phrase is made of words from a standardized 2,048-word list. Each word corresponds to a unique index number (1–2048), and each index can be represented as a 12-bit binary number. This is what gets punched into the plate.

01

The BIP39 Word List

Your wallet generates a seed phrase from a standardized dictionary of 2,048 English words. Each word has a fixed position (index) in the list.

03

Convert to Binary

The dictionary already shows the 12-bit binary representation for every word. Each bit position corresponds to a power of 2. Filled = 1, empty = 0.

Punch the Plate

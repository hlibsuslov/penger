[ THE PROTOCOL ]

Precision in Every Punch.

A BIP39 seed phrase is fundamentally a number system. Penger v1.0 doesn’t just record words; it archives them as raw, mathematical data.

  1. 01

    Locate

    Find each word of your 12-word or 24-word recovery phrase in the provided printed dictionary.

  2. 02

    Punch

    Use the included puncher to mark the plate corresponding to your words. Every row is one word. You do this entirely offline.

  3. 03

    Calculate & Recover

    To read the backup in the future, simply calculate the total for each row. The sum gives you the exact index number in the BIP39 word list.
